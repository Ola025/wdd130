Lagos, Nigeria

Lagos is the largest city in Nigeria and one of the fastest-growing cities in the world. It is known for its vibrant culture, busy markets, and beautiful Atlantic coastline.

The city is full of music, food, and diverse communities, making it one of the most exciting places to live and visit in Africa.
